About Me

I'm a third-year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Audisankara College of Engineering & Technology, Gudur, Andhra Pradesh. My academic journey has taken me deep into VLSI design — from Boolean logic and K-maps to OpenLANE RTL-to-GDSII flows and FPGA architecture.

Alongside hardware, I've developed a strong foundation in software quality assurance, working with Selenium WebDriver, TestNG, and the Page Object Model for Java-based automation testing. I believe robust engineering requires both: systems designed right, and systems tested right.

When I'm not at a workstation, I'm following IPL cricket, Telugu cinema, or exploring the local culture of Kavali — grounded, curious, and always learning.
